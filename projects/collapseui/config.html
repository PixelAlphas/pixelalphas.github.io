Customizability

A section for customize the way UI should be.

Greetings!

This is Collapse-UI's Customizability tab and are only deem fit with people who wish to modify Collapse-UI's look and feel.


You can use it for making a theme, layout changes, etc without altering the main pack. pretty useful if you wanna use pre-config for pvp or survival etc.


In the future this pack will become even more relevant as Collapse-UI progress, probably like adjustable hud positions, and probably a lot more. (Not planned, but may in the foreseen future be considered and added.)


You'll need an Config Template pack that can be downloaded here or Installation tab.
